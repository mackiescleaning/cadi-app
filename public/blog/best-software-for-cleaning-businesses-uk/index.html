The "stack of tools" problem

Question: Why do cleaning businesses use so many different apps?

Because until recently, there wasn't a single platform that handled everything well. So cleaning businesses ended up with:

  • A booking form on their website (often a basic form builder)
  • A separate invoicing tool (FreeAgent, QuickBooks, or just Word)
  • WhatsApp for team communications
  • Google Sheets for scheduling
  • A manual process for chasing reviews

This creates real problems:

Data duplication: client details entered once in the booking system need to be entered again in invoicing. Every manual duplication is a potential error.

Blind spots: if your booking system doesn't talk to your invoicing system, you can easily invoice the wrong amount or miss an invoice entirely.

Client experience gaps: a client who rebooks through one system but whose invoice comes from another looks disorganised. Small businesses lose clients to this kind of thing.

Time cost: the admin overhead of managing multiple tools is significant. Every tool has its own login, its own interface, its own support process.
